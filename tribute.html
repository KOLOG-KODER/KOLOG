    "Who is this book for? To anyone who is either in a mess or suffering or has lost track in life. This is also a wonderful read if you want to understand the behind-the-scenes of why some people behave the way they do. You can also read this to better equip yourself in helping those around you. If you are not a reader and especially not familiar with Prof. Peterson's work, then I would not recommend this book to you. You can instead read the summary of this book which is, surprisingly, written by Prof. Peterson himself. If you hate reading, you can watch an hour and 30 minutes of Prof. Peterson's lecture where he explains all the rules in satisfactory detail. The lecture is available on a channel called "How To Academy" on YouTube. If you are familiar with Prof. Peterson's work, then you know how complex his sentence structures and vocabulary are. He, sometimes, delves into details so much that I almost forgot what rule he was discussing. I had to flip pages backward and remind myself. This is my biggest complaint. Being the person he is, Prof. Peterson has tons of Biblical stories and references that he uses to get his point across. If you are a Christian or a Muslim, you will understand most of the stories and comprehend his points instantly. However, to others, it might just sound gibberish. There are occasions where some of the things really hit you where it hurts. He lays out the truth naked in front of you and does not go easy with the harsh truths. The book is also structured incredibly well unlike many other self-help books. So, yeah, that was a bonus. It took me around 20 days to understand and appreciate that every word in this book is placed exactly where it's supposed to be. The book is packed with information. I would suggest you take it at a slow pace to get the maximum benefit."

    --THE MULTI-MILLION COPY BESTSELLER
